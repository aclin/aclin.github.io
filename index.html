Why is this page so simple?

If you're looking at this page and wondering why it's so simplistic, the answer is that it's completely intentional.

Here are my objectives with this page:

  1. You should be able to read this page without any distractions.
  2. No bells and whistles to distract you. No trite sunset/cool tech/ironic hero images or "deep" quotes to bore you or get in your way.
  3. You're probably reading, or will read this on your phone at some point. This page should load just as quickly on either without you scrolling very far to get to the point.
